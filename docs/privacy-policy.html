Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 March 2026

Life in the UK ("the App") is operated by Quantara Technologies ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we handle it.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a password (stored securely using industry-standard hashing). This is required to save your progress and sync across devices.

Learning Progress Data: We store data related to your use of the App, including:

  • Quiz and mock exam answers and scores
  • Study progress and topics read
  • Spaced repetition card states
  • Starred/bookmarked questions
  • Flashcard progress
  • Practice test results
  • Achievement unlocks and XP earned
  • Daily streak data

Subscription Information: If you purchase a premium subscription, payment is processed entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment card details. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status.

Error and Performance Data: We use Sentry (a third-party error tracking service) to collect anonymous crash reports and performance data. This helps us fix bugs and improve the App. No personally identifiable information is included in these reports.

Device Information: We may collect basic device information (device type, operating system version) solely for the purpose of ensuring app compatibility and diagnosing technical issues.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your location data
  • We do not collect your contacts or phone number
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, or photos
  • We do not serve advertisements or share data with advertisers
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Save and sync your learning progress across devices
  • Track your study streaks and achievements
  • Calculate your exam readiness score
  • Process premium subscription status
  • Fix bugs and improve app performance
  • Send password reset emails when requested

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in two places:

  • On your device: Learning progress is stored locally using SQLite so the App works offline.
  • In the cloud: When you have an internet connection, data is synced to our cloud database hosted by Supabase, with servers located in the United Kingdom (London, eu-west-2).

We implement appropriate security measures including Row Level Security (RLS) on all user data tables, encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), and secure token storage on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase (authentication and data storage) – Privacy Policy
  • Sentry (error tracking) – Privacy Policy
  • Google Play (app distribution and in-app purchases) – Privacy Policy
  • Expo / EAS (app updates) – Privacy Policy

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete all your personal data from our servers within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated data (such as total number of users) may be retained indefinitely.

7. Your Rights

Under UK data protection law (UK GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you believe your rights have been violated

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is designed as an educational tool for individuals preparing for the Life in the UK test. It is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting a notice within the App. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
