Delete Your Account

How to delete your Life in the UK app account and all associated data

Delete from within the App

1

Open the Life in the UK app and go to the Profile tab.

2

Scroll down and tap Delete Account.

3

Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Delete via Email

If you cannot access the App, you can request account deletion by emailing us at ateeq.pirzada01@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account. Include "Delete My Account" in the subject line.

Please note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All of the following data will be permanently deleted within 30 days:

Data that will be deleted

  • Your account and profile information
  • Quiz and mock exam history
  • Study progress and topics read
  • Spaced repetition card data
  • Starred/bookmarked questions
  • Flashcard progress
  • Practice test results
  • Achievements and XP
  • Daily streak records
  • Subscription and usage data

If you have an active premium subscription, please cancel it through Google Play before deleting your account to avoid continued billing.
