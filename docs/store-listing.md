## Full Description

Prepare for the Life in the UK test with confidence. Life in the UK Test 2026 is the most comprehensive study app designed specifically for people applying for British citizenship or settlement.

Whether you're studying for the first time or revising before your test date, this app gives you everything you need to pass — all in one place.

STUDY MATERIAL
All 21 study topics from the Home Office handbook, broken down into clear, easy-to-read topics. Track your reading progress as you work through each chapter.

PRACTICE QUIZZES
Test your knowledge with chapter-by-chapter quizzes. Get instant feedback on every answer with clear explanations so you understand the material, not just memorise it.

MOCK EXAMS
Sit realistic 24-question mock exams that mirror the real test format. Get your score instantly and see where you need to improve. Take as many mock exams as you want.

FLASHCARDS
Study key facts, dates, and people with interactive flashcards powered by spaced repetition. The app tracks which cards you know well and which need more practice, so you study smarter.

PROGRESS TRACKING
See your readiness score at a glance. Track quiz scores, mock exam results, study progress, and daily streaks to stay motivated and on track.

DAILY STREAKS & ACHIEVEMENTS
Build a study habit with daily streaks and unlock achievements as you progress. Stay motivated with XP rewards for every study session.

OFFLINE ACCESS
Study anywhere, even without an internet connection. All content is stored on your device so you can revise on the bus, train, or during your lunch break.

SYNC ACROSS DEVICES
Create a free account to save your progress and sync it across all your devices. Start studying on your phone and continue on your tablet.

- 1000+ practice questions with explanations
